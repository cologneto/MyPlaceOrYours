My Place or Yours?

That is the question. Wanna come up to me? Of prefer to stay at home? That's boring, don't do that. You are

coming to an exciting city like Budapest, so come up to mine, and stay at a cool place.


My place - or hopefully yours - is a set of apartments in the heart of Budapest, a short walk to the most interest

ing sights and things to do here. The nightlife area with the famous ruin bars are just across the main road from us.

Gellért and Rudas baths are less than 15 minutes walk, or a few minutes on public transport. Keleti train station is

very near as well, only three stops on the bus.


Our apartments are designed to satisfy your visual and comfort needs. We play with colours, but we are serious

about variety. We provide breakfast, partnered with Torino Café, where Marco, the Italian owner makes the best

coffee in town. Your breakfast is free if you book directly on this website. You can park your car in the nearby

parking lot. Free of charge as well if you book directly with us.


We operate an airport shuttle service, for as little as 19 Euros for a full car. That's not per person, that is for up to

4 people in the car, you can hardly beat that.

An Airport Shuttle Service


We operate an airport shuttle service, for as little as 19 Euros.You will be collected by an English-speaking chauffeur and driven safely to your destination in comfort and privacy.

To use the airport shuttle service, you will need a mobile phone that works in Hungary. When you will have passed the passport control and collected your luggage, as you leave the terminal you will have to call the driver and he will pick you up in the lower parking lot outside the terminal. Just call him, walk downstairs he will be there then.

To reserve a transfer from the airport to your hotel, please complete the booking form below, or contact us directly.
